Bonzo

First Phase

Bonzo will continually summon Undead(50k HP), and run around attacking players with wither skulls during his first phase. Bonzo has 250k HP and will move on to the second phase once players have killed his first phase.

Second Phase

Bonzo revives during his second phase, healing himself back to his max health and gaining a melee attack and Balloon Barrage:Bonzo moves to the center of the arena before unleashing a barrage of balloons around him in all directions.
