What is Skyblock??

Hypixel Skyblock is an economy base grinding game often compared to an MMORPG (massively multiplayer online roleplaying game). It's a game mode within the server Hypixel, where rather than fighting for an object like other mini-games the players create their own objective within massive maps created within Minecraft.

What are Dungeon's?

Dungeons are a procedurally generated map in which players have to clear Whither rooms to get a key to move forwards. As you keep moving further inwards to get to what is called the Blood Room in which you have to kill tough enemies. Once Blood room is clear a portal Opens talking to the boss of that Floor.

Each floor completion grants you xp for your Catacombs skill while killing monsters gives you xp towards you combat skill. The higher level the floor is the higher the required Catacombs level

Classes

In the Catacombs you can enter each floor in groups of up to five, there are also five different classes for each person to choose from each filling a different roll. Each class has a class-specific buff and abilities within the dungeon, those being a passive, Ultimate and adn normal ability. If there is only one person set to a class they also receive a 100% boost to the class buffs.

Berserker

The berserker class focuses on the close-quarters aspect of fighting and so the buffs reflect that style boosting movement speed and melee damage.
One class passive is called Bloodlust: After killing a monster you receive damage boost to your next melee attack.
Throwing Axe: Throw an Axe, dealing the same damage as your height hit in the last minute. Cooldown: 10 seconds
Ragnarok: Big boost to movement speed and attack speed and a 1.5x damage boost. It also summons 3 zombie minions to ade in your battle Cooldown: 60 seconds

Archer

As can be inferred by the name the archer class focusing on damage done using the few boys that the game has to offer.
Doubleshoot: a 50% chance to shoot another arrow
Explosive Shot: Shoots a volley of three arrows the explode on impact, dealing the same damage as your highest bow hit in the last minute in a four block radius. Cooldown: 40 seconds
Rapid Fire: Shoots five arrows per second for five seconds!Cooldown: 100 Seconds

Mage

Mage being the last of the damaging classes focusing on the abilities of the weapon the use to deal the damage.
Mage Staff: All your melee weapon attacks become ranged. Those attacks deal 30% of their melee Damage (increased by your Intelligence) up to 10 blocks
Guided Sheep: Shoots a Guided Sheep, dealing Damage based on your Mage level! Cooldown: 30 Seconds
Thunderstorm: Unleash a Thunderstorm, striking enemies in a 10 block radius for 15 seconds, dealing Damage based on your Mage level! Cooldown: 500 Seconds

Tank

The tank is built to take damage foe the team an so there passive help them achieve that
Diversion: Divert 80% of the Damage taken by your teammates within 30 blocks around you.
Seismic Wave: A Seismic Wave emerges from underneath you and travels in a straight line. Deals 20000 + 10% Damage for every +50 Defense to any enemy in its path. Cooldown: 15 Seconds
Castle of Stone: Fortifies your defense, reducing the Damage you take by 70% for 20 seconds. Also, aggro's all enemies in a 10 block radius. Cooldown: 150 Seconds

Healer

The healers class is self-explanatory
Healing Aura: Passively heals all living teammates within a 8 block radius for 1% HP per second.
Healing Circle: Launches a healing orb that creates a healing circle where it lands. The healing circle heals friendly players inside for 2% of their Health every second. Cooldown: 2 Seconds
Wish: Heals everyone in your group to full health and grants them a shields for 20% of their maximum health. The cooldown is reduced by 10 seconds for every player below 25% health. Cooldown: 120 Seconds
